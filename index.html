MELBOURNE HOUSE PRICES

The map in Figure 1 shows the average house prices in each Local Government Area (LGA) normalised by the number of bedrooms within each house. The average price per bedroom in the map looks to increase the closer to the city centre it is. This is shown by the LGA which the highest average house price is Stonnington with an average of AUD$833,333 per bedroom. Additionally the LGA with the lowest average price per bedroom is Wyndham with an average of AUD$128,000 per bedroom.

Figure 1: Choropleth map of Melbourne of average house price normalised by number of bedrooms.

This plot, Figure 2, shows what to expect in terms of house prices for the number of bedrooms. A selector at the bottom of the page can be used to change the LGA considered. The lowest points in each column represent the minimum price a house has been sold for in the area considered. The blue region is the interquatile range which is the region in which 50% of the houses considered have been sold for the number of bedrooms, with a white line representing the median house price. The maximum line encloses the Price range of 95% of houses sold for that number of bedrooms, the points outside of this area show outlyers which should not be considered unless looking for a house with many luxuries.

In both charts the number in the middle is the total number of houses sold in the area considered. The left chart shows the percentage number of methods houses have been sold in the area considered. The Second chart shows the types of houses sold. House includes: House; Cottage; Villa; Semi; Terrace. Unit inclused: Unit; Duplex. Townhouse incluses: Townhouse.
